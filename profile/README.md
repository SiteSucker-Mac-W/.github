## 🖌 Overview  
SiteSucker Advanced is a website downloading and archiving application that retrieves complete websites for offline browsing by automatically following links and downloading every page, image, stylesheet, script, and linked resource it encounters.

The download engine recursively follows links from the starting URL, building a complete local copy of a site that maintains the original structure and relative link relationships so that navigation works offline exactly as it does online.

Depth and domain limiting controls prevent runaway downloads from following links to external sites or going more levels deep than necessary, keeping downloads focused on the specific site or section you want to archive.

Scheduled downloading automates the capture of sites that update regularly, building a versioned archive of a site's content at configured intervals without requiring manual intervention for each capture.

For researchers, developers, and web archivists who need reliable offline copies of websites for reference, compliance, or preservation purposes, SiteSucker Advanced is the downloading tool that handles the complete capture automatically and accurately.
